Habitat conditions over the years has steadily declined.![Black-rumped Flameback](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-6-1024x683_f_improf_604x403.BcmfrFox_Z2b84ri.webp) In many coffee zones, decline in habitat conditions is due to excess of precipitation during the summer or winter months and drought like conditions during the monsoon period.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (2)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-2-1024x683.DPrHnTDl_1kKxWO.webp) These climatic changes has profoundly impacted the fruit set in many wild fruit bearing trees. It has also had a chain effect.![Glossy Ibis](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-3-1024x683_f_improf_604x403.C7SCU-oY_ZxM8Kl.webp) Unfortunately, the future does not look promising for birds. As climate impacts birds and other wildlife, they have to move to uncharted territory and adapt quickly to the changing situations.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (4)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-4-1024x683.Cc01OpuN_ZPgRba.webp) In most cases lack of food, water, predators and dwindling natural resources compound the problem.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (7)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-7-1024x683.Pf3jpQib_1OWF19.webp) We are certain that similar problems exist in every corner of the globe.![Black Headed Ibis](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/s-14-1024x683.D74YUQp6_Z2hA5bY.webp) The Earth is undergoing profound changes to its climate.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (9)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-9-1024x683.BpaSZ9CC_ZfsRlV.webp) Global warming has raised the planet’s average surface temperature about 1.5 degrees Fahrenheit since the Industrial revolution.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (10)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-10-1024x683.DYu9YW7e_2rWvOK.webp) This impact on Climate Change is largely driven by human greed rather than human need.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (11)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-11.C8it4ZPI_340d4.webp) In our opinion, Humans are directly responsible for most of the threats to wildlife. Some of these threats include habitat loss, land degradation and fragmentation, pollution and overexploitation of the Planet’s finite natural resources.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (12)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-12-1024x683.BKG0-Yrg_1QJgM4.webp) In the coming years, human induced global warming may be the most serious threat, not only to wildlife but to all of humanity, jeopardizing the life sustaining ability of our Green Planet .![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (13)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-13-1024x683.B8NwZDv9_ZJOvVh.webp) Bird populations around the world are excellent bio indicators which provide crucial insights into the effects of climate change on ecosystems.![Stork-billed Kingfisher](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-14-1024x683_f_improf_604x403.DieRniX3_Z1d4ARf.webp) All species of birds appear to be very sensitive to changes in climate, and evidence seems to indicate that this sensitivity makes them primary indicators of global climate change.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (15)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/s-9-1024x682.DBB6aklw_Z8D74X.webp) They are among the best studied and monitored for changes occurring due to the impact of global warming. Scientists have found declines of up to 90% in some bird populations, as well as total and unprecedented reproductive failure in others.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (16)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-16-1024x683.D7_UFU-d_1hxtGI.webp) Currently, there are 10,425 known living species of birds, 21 percent of which are currently prone to extinction due to a wide variety of threats.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (17)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-17-1024x683.BxHEF3PD_1coDFb.webp) Climate change is already having a strong negative impact on birds, affecting bird’s behaviour, distribution and population dynamics across Europe, say a group of scientists publishing their findings to create the world’s first indicator of the climate change impacts on wildlife at a continental scale.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (18)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-18-1024x683.BcyRCfS1_Z1f1AJq.webp) More than 350 of the Planet’s most important sites for nature are threatened according to a new report by Bird Life International. Bird and biodiversity areas are places of International significance for the conservation of the world’s birds.![Greater Egret](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-19-1024x683.BGoASaGt_Z2bzWgy.webp) In the Indian context, 10 important bird and biodiversity areas (IBA’s) are in grave danger, especially the Western Ghats bird endemic areas.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (20)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-20-1024x683.CvwU4RCw_167QTQ.webp) It’s only going to get worse, since extreme weather events are becoming more severe.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (21)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-21-1024x683.BHCgNAyy_6dKwp.webp) Many birds are forced to shift their predictable ranges to survive and in this battle of quick adaptation, one can often see more losers than winners, resulting in significant loss of bird species which are already threatened or fail to adapt to a new environment.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (22)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-22-1024x683.BQxQpV2N_18fyvX.webp) Impact of climate change has an interesting paradox too. On one hand, the endangered and rare species suffer but on the other some species may benefit.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (23)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-23-1024x683.bLqBibH5_ZzkYlk.webp) Both resident and migratory birds considered relatively safe may also suffer decline in populations. As the climate continues to change, some species won’t be able to find suitable habitats.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (24)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-24-1024x683.CdU_mHqU_Z1FR9KY.webp) ### **Effects of Climate Change**- Birds are exposed to Harsh winters making it difficult for them to survive.![2015 Aug. Shade &amp;#x26; future of birds- (26)](https://ecofriendlycoffee.org/assets/2015-Aug.-Shade-future-of-birds-26-1024x697.B9MGiZy4_Thwcu.webp) - Wetlands which often act as refueling stops for migratory birds may vanish resulting in food shortage![2015 Aug.
